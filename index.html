A bit about me

Data engineer by trade,
problem-solver by nature.

I moved to the US from India to do my Master's in Data Science at the University of Memphis — and data engineering kind of claimed me along the way. There's something deeply satisfying about taking a chaotic pile of raw data and turning it into something a business can actually act on.

My day-to-day involves designing pipelines on Azure (Databricks, ADLS, ADF), building medallion architectures, and making sure data gets where it needs to go — clean, on time, and trustworthy. I care a lot about data quality, not just data volume.

Outside of work, I'm the person who gets irrationally excited when a SQL query runs 50% faster after a few index tweaks. Small wins, big impact — that's the philosophy I bring to every project.
